How Every Review Is Produced

Every supplement on FitLabReviews goes through the same fixed 5-step process before a score is assigned. No shortcuts, no brand exceptions.

Step 01
Ingredient Analysis
Every ingredient checked against peer-reviewed literature for efficacy, bioavailability, and safety at the dose used.
Step 02
Dosing Accuracy
Each dose compared against clinically effective amounts from research. Underdosed ingredients are flagged and penalised.
Step 03
Label Transparency
Proprietary blends lose points. Full disclosure of every ingredient and dose is the standard we hold products to.
Step 04
Real-World Testing
Products tested personally where possible — taste, mixability, and actual effect over 4–8 weeks factored in.
Step 05
Value Assessment
Cost per effective dose calculated and compared to alternatives. Great formulas that cost 3× more are penalised accordingly.

How FitLab Scores Are Calculated
